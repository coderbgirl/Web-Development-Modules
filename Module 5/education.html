Primary school - Little Flowers

I compeleted my primary education in Little flowers school. My days at this school were very good. I was able to make many friends here and the 8 years I spent here shaped my personality. I enjoyed every moment here.

Well almost every moment, to be precise.
The reason for that was my History class. I hated History. I pretty much nailed Basic Sciences and Math but History was my Achilles' heel.

I was very active student that time. I participated in almost every extra curricular activity and I played many sports like kho-kho, hand ball, kabbadi etc. Eventally, I became the captain of a few athletics teams, while keeping a strong focus on my studies. Of all the great memories, my favourite was the one when I got the 1st prize in 400 meter race at state level. My parents, teachers and classmates were very proud. I got many prizes from my school and other government organizations. I guess that was best time of my life.


Higher education - Panchsheel Vidhyalaya

After primary school, I finished my high school education in Panchsheel Vidhyalaya.

Compared to my primary school life, my higher studies were very differnt. I took science as my major. In my high school, I was primarly focused on study and less on sports. All courses were my fun during high school, but my favourite course was Math.

Ashok Patel was my Math teacher. Whenever I got a complex Math problem, he used some tricks to covert it into simple one. I used to call that class, a fun class. In India, final exam of high school, which is known as "Board Exam", plays an important role in college admission. I worked so hard and secured 91% in my board exam and I got admission in my dream college. That was my best memory of high school.
